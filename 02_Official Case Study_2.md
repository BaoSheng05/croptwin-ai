# UTMxHackathon’26 Official Case Study
**Case Study 1: Precision Urban Agriculture for Vertical Farming**[cite: 11]

## Introduction
Vertical farming offers a space-efficient, sustainable alternative by growing crops in stacked layers within controlled indoor environments to address urbanization and climate change[cite: 11].

## Problem Statement
Manual management is resource-intensive and prone to human error[cite: 11]. Fluctuating climate control and improper nutrient delivery lead to suboptimal yields, wasted water, and high energy consumption[cite: 11].

## The Challenge
Develop an intelligent, automated system to monitor and manage environmental variables in real-time[cite: 11]. The platform must collect sensor data, automate routine tasks, and provide data-driven insights to maximize yield while minimizing resource usage[cite: 11].

## Key Features for Solution
*   **Real-time Monitoring**: Temperature, humidity, soil moisture, and water pH[cite: 11].
*   **Automated Control**: Adjust LED spectrums, fans, or hydroponic pumps based on plant profiles[cite: 11].
*   **Dashboard**: Visualize growth metrics, resource consumption, and trends[cite: 11].
*   **Predictive Alerts**: Notify users of system anomalies or dry reservoirs[cite: 11].
*   **AI Recommendation Engine**: Suggest optimal harvest times or nutrient adjustments[cite: 11].

## Recommended Tools
*   **Hardware**: Arduino, ESP32, Raspberry Pi, Sensors[cite: 11].
*   **Backend**: Node.js, Python (FastAPI/Flask), Firebase, Supabase[cite: 11].
*   **Frontend**: React, Vue.js, Flutter, React Native[cite: 11].
*   **AI/Data**: TensorFlow Lite, Scikit-learn, OpenAI API[cite: 11].
*   **Communication**: MQTT, WebSockets, REST APIs[cite: 11].
